About Us

We are Feeding Nigeria and we are ready to take on two of the most grievious Problems in Nigeria; one of those Problems is the food-wastage and the other is the Lack of food for many Families. Many Families go to Bed with nothing in their stomachs and we intent to help them out wth that

Our Beginning

Our Founder and CEO Dynasty first came up with this idea while participating in an Hackathon(a competitive event for software engineers) and he began to research a lot of stuffs and found out how many Nigerians are suffering and at the same time, Many Nigerians waste a lot of foods; and thus, Feeding Nigeria was born.

Our Mission

Dynasty(Founder and CEO): No doubt about it, Poverty, Hunger cannot be completely curbed but if we @Feeding Nigeria can do our little best, we should be able to reduce Hunger and food-wastage to a small percentage. And we are not Tribal biased but we travel accross different Nigerian Tribes

Our Vision

If we can ensure that 65% Household in Nigeria have at least a plate of food in a day, then we know we can combat Hunger in Nigeria and at the same time combat Poverty in our beloved Country Nigeria.

Our Mandate

Anywhere we go to, any tribe we visit in Nigeria we ensure we leave no stone untouched. Our Mandate is that there must and should be zero complains related to how we served Nigerians. At every event, Everyone must be fed

Our Plans

To feed as many Nigerians as Possible, and to eradicate Poverty and Hunger by giving out loands to Nigerians with business ideas, equipping the Youths, and Adults with Life-coping skills, Speaking to Kids about bullying. All this and many more is what we intend to do in the Future
